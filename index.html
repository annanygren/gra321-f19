    • A Quick Look at the Brandenburg Gate

    The Brandenburg gate was built between 1788 and 1791. It was created to be the key entry point to Berlin by Purssian Kind Frederick William II. The gate is topped by a statue known as the Quadriga, which is a statue of the goddess of victory driving a chariot pulled by four horses.
